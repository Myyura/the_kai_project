### 1.

$$
\begin{aligned}
\lim_{x \to 0} \left( \frac{a^x + b^x}{2} \right)^{\frac{1}{x}}
&= \lim_{x \to 0} \left( 1 + \frac{\log (ab)}{2} x + O(x^2) \right)^{\frac{1}{x}}
\\
&= e^{\frac{1}{2} \log (ab)}
\\
&= \sqrt{ab}
\end{aligned}
$$
